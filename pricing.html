Membership Options

Experience the excitement and innovation of the Space Symposium with our exclusive membership options!

Whether you are an industry professional, an enthusiast, or just curious about the future of space, we have a membership package designed just for you.

Platinum Member

  • Full Access to All Events for Each Day: Experience the symposium in its entirety, with access to every event throughout the four-day program.
  • Exclusive Formal Dinners: Attend formal evening dinners to connect with keynote speakers, industry leaders, and fellow enthusiasts in a refined setting.

Price: £350 (Unlock the ultimate Space Symposium experience and enjoy exclusive networking opportunities.)

Gold Member

  • Full Access to All Events on Each Day: Take part in every session, workshop, and keynote speech across the four days of the symposium.
  • Premium Seating: Secure prime seating in all events for the best view of our esteemed speakers.

Price: £250 (Experience the full spectrum of the symposium and maximize your participation!)

Silver Member

  • Full Access to All Events Over Two Days: Choose two days of the symposium to attend, and immerse yourself in the most engaging sessions and workshops.
  • Customized Experience: Tailor your attendance to the most relevant days for your interests.

Price: £150 (Great for those who want to experience the symposium without committing to the full four days!)

Day Pass

  • Full Access to All Events Over a Day: Pick any day of the symposium and participate in all events, including public sessions, workshops, and keynote speeches.
  • Flexibility: Perfect for those with limited availability who still want to be part of the symposium experience.

Price: £100 (A perfect choice for a taste of the symposium on your chosen day!)

Dinner Ticket

  • Access to Any Formal Evening Dinner: Enjoy a formal dinner with keynote speakers, industry experts, and peers.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with leaders in the space industry over a memorable evening meal.

Price: £125 (A great opportunity to mingle and dine with influential figures in the industry!)

Single Session Pass

  • Access to Any Single Public Session or Workshop: Attend a specific session or workshop of your choice.
  • Customize Your Experience: Choose the session that best matches your interests or professional focus.

Price: £35 per session (A cost-effective way to engage with the symposium and expand your knowledge in a specific area!)
